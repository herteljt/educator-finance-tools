About This Project

These tools are part of the Personal Finance for Educators project — a Substack and podcast focused on helping K–12 teachers build financial stability on a teacher's salary. Each tool is free, runs entirely in your browser, and requires no account or login. Questions or suggestions? Find me on Substack.
